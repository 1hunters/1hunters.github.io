Chen Tang

I am currently a research assistant in the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Tsinghua University, under the supervision of Prof. Wenwu Zhu and Prof. Zhi Wang. I hold a Master's degree in Computer Technology from Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Zhi Wang. I also work closely with Prof. Yifei Zhu from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) and Dr. Li Lyna Zhang from Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA). My research interests are in efficient deep learning (e.g., quantization, sparsity), hardware-aware neural architecture search, and dynamic neural networks.

Previously, I was a visiting student at AIoT Lab, Institute for AI Industry Research (AIR), Tsinghua University. Before that, I was a research intern in System and Networking Research Group of MSRA and was a visiting student of Peng Cheng National Laboratory (PCL). I have served as a reviewer for CVPR, ECCV, ECAI, and NeurIPS.
